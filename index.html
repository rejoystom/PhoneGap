First page header

A header can be added to a page by adding a div with a data-role="header".

Notice how the header scrolls with your content. You can change this by adding the data-position="fixed" to your header.

Similarly, a footer can be created by adding a div with data-role="footer". In this page it already has the data-position="fixed" so it does not scroll out of view.
