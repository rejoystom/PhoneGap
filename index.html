jQuery Mobile is built around the concept of pages: units of content and navigation.

Pages are defined by adding the "data-role" attribute to a div and defining an id. Inside a page we can have different types of elements, but the main content goes into a div with a "class" of "ui-content"

We can allow users to navigate through pages simply by inserting a link to the id of the page
